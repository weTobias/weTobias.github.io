LEAGUE OF

LEGENDS

League of Legends, or LoL, is a free to play MOBA game that is available for Windows and macOS. With its 10 years of age, it is one of the biggest eSports titles with millions of regular viewers and one of the biggest videogames in general. The main goal of the game is to destroy the enemy nexus in a 5v5 arena, each player playing one of the ove 140 champions, with each having different abilitys and items to buy. The main map, beeing "Summoners Rift", is composed of 3 "lanes" a jungle and many othe things. LoL is a highly mechanical and strategical game where you always have room to improve and new things to learn. The huge playerbase won't make it easy for you.
